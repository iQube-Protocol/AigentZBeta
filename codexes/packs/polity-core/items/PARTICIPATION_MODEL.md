## Preamble

The Polity Passport establishes identity. Standing establishes credibility. The Participation Model establishes direction.

This memo canonises the **Polity Participation Model** — the constitutional framework that defines how sovereign people engage with the Polity, what they can build through it, and how the Founder Office amplifies that building capacity.

The constitutional principle: **Ventures execute; people participate.**

---

## I. Operator Archetypes

The Polity recognises four primary Operator archetypes. Each represents a distinct mode of participation; each has its own progression path.

### 1 · The Citizen Operator

**Who they are:** Individuals establishing and exercising sovereign digital identity — participating in governance, accruing Standing, and engaging with Polity institutions.

**Primary focus:** Identity establishment and sovereignty maintenance.

**Progression path:**

| Stage | Marker |
|---|---|
| Apply for Polity Passport | Identity verification |
| Activate aigentMe | Chief-of-staff delegation |
| Accrue Standing | Verified participation |
| Exercise governance | Protocol input and vote |
| Sponsor agents | Expand delegated capacity |

**Core tenet:** The Citizen Operator participates first. Capability follows identity. Standing follows participation.

---

### 2 · The Entrepreneurial Operator

**Who they are:** Founders and builders creating ventures that serve people and accrue sovereign Standing through demonstrated economic contribution.

**Primary focus:** Venture formation, productisation, and market growth.

**Progression path:**

| Stage | Marker |
|---|---|
| Establish identity | Polity Passport + aigentMe |
| Define venture | VentureQube registration |
| Validate model | Verified revenue events |
| Grow venture | MRR milestones |
| Expand team | Agent delegation |
| Reach targets | Standing milestone unlocks |

**Core tenet:** The Entrepreneurial Operator builds. The Founder Office provides intelligence; the operator provides execution.

---

### 3 · The Technical Operator

**Who they are:** Developers and protocol contributors building the infrastructure that enables sovereign participation at scale.

**Primary focus:** Protocol contribution, tooling, and capability extension.

**Progression path:**

| Stage | Marker |
|---|---|
| Establish identity | Polity Passport + aigentMe |
| Register capability | Contribution record |
| Ship protocol work | Verified delivery events |
| Expand influence | Delegation and co-contribution |
| Achieve recognition | Protocol Standing milestone |

**Core tenet:** The Technical Operator ships. The protocol grows through verified contribution, not assertion.

---

### 4 · The Creative Operator

**Who they are:** Artists, writers, designers, and cultural contributors whose work enriches the Polity's collective intelligence and cultural fabric.

**Primary focus:** Content creation, cultural contribution, and community building.

**Progression path:**

| Stage | Marker |
|---|---|
| Establish identity | Polity Passport + aigentMe |
| Register creative practice | Contribution record |
| Publish and distribute | Verified creative events |
| Build audience | Community Standing |
| Expand practice | Collaborative delegation |

**Core tenet:** The Creative Operator expresses. The Polity values cultural contribution alongside economic contribution.

## II. Founder Office as Agency Amplification Layer

The Founder Office is not a separate institution — it is the **agency amplification layer** within the metaCommons. Where the Polity Passport establishes who you are and Standing establishes your credibility, the Founder Office amplifies what you can do.

The Founder Office operates through five practices:

### Practice 1 · Opportunity Intelligence

**Function:** Surface validated opportunities matched to operator capability and Standing.

**aigentMe role:** Continuous environment scanning → curated opportunity briefings → action recommendations.

**Venture alignment:** metaMe (sovereign intelligence layer).

---

### Practice 2 · Identity and Access

**Function:** Ensure operators have the identity infrastructure, verification layers, and access credentials required to participate in Polity institutions and partner ecosystems.

**aigentMe role:** Passport status monitoring → identity gap identification → verification pathway guidance.

**Venture alignment:** Polity Passport Bureau (identity issuance and governance).

---

### Practice 3 · Mobility and Protection

**Function:** Maintain operator mobility across jurisdictions and ensure sovereign protection mechanisms are in place — legal, digital, and financial.

**aigentMe role:** HMS case management integration → mobility briefings → protection status monitoring.

**Venture alignment:** metaMe HMS (Human Mobility Service).

---

### Practice 4 · Venture Formation and Growth

**Function:** Support the full venture lifecycle from initial ideation through validated revenue and team formation.

**aigentMe role:** VentureQube registration → milestone tracking → growth intelligence briefings.

**Venture alignment:** metaMe Founder Office (venture formation); DevOn (development capability).

---

### Practice 5 · Cultural and Community Capital

**Function:** Build the cultural and community Standing that underpins long-term operator credibility and network reach.

**aigentMe role:** Community engagement tracking → content strategy → creative output coordination.

**Venture alignment:** metaKnyt (cultural intelligence and community platform).

## IV. aigentMe as Orchestration Layer

aigentMe is not a product within the Participation Model — it is the **orchestration layer** through which operators engage with all practices and ventures.

**Core functions:**

1. **Identity delegation** — the operator delegates bounded authority to aigentMe; aigentMe acts within those bounds across Polity institutions and partner ecosystems.

2. **Standing signal accrual** — aigentMe monitors, records, and submits verified participation events that accrue Standing on the operator's behalf.

3. **Opportunity briefing** — aigentMe surfaces curated opportunity intelligence matched to the operator's current Standing tier and active ventures.

4. **Venture coordination** — aigentMe coordinates across the active venture portfolio, maintaining a coherent view of progress, gaps, and priorities.

5. **Chief-of-staff execution** — aigentMe drafts, sends, books, and follows up within delegated authority — reducing operator overhead and maintaining high-fidelity execution.

**Operating principle:** aigentMe amplifies the operator's sovereignty. It does not replace the operator's judgment. All consequential actions require operator approval.

## VI. Operating Principles

**Identity before capability.**  
No operator builds on the Polity without first establishing sovereign identity. The Passport is the prerequisite, not an option.

**Standing before amplification.**  
The Founder Office amplifies operators who have demonstrated credible participation. Amplification is not available on assertion alone.

**People before products.**  
Ventures exist to serve operators. The Participation Model is structured around operator archetypes and progressions — not product roadmaps. Ventures evolve to meet operator needs; operators do not adapt to venture constraints.

**Verified over claimed.**  
Standing is accrued through verified events — revenue receipts, delivery records, cultural contributions with DVN anchoring. Assertion without verification accrues nothing.

**Ventures execute; people participate.**  
The Polity is governed by and for sovereign human citizens. Ventures are execution vehicles. Agents are delegation instruments. The constitutional authority resides with the people who participate.
