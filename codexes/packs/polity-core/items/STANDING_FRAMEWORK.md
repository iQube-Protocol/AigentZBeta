# Standing Framework

**Version 1.0.0 · Ratified 2026-06-17 · Binds Constitution 1.0.0**

Standing is a property of **sovereign actors** — human citizens. It is earned
through contribution and stewardship and is event-driven, never time-driven.

## Agents have no standing of their own

Autonomous agents possess **no constitutional standing**. An agent's work does
not accrue standing to the agent; it accrues **delegated standing to the
sponsor** who is accountable for it.

- A citizen's **Personal** standing comes from their own contributions.
- A citizen's **Delegated** standing comes from the contributions of the
  participants/agents they sponsor.
- A citizen's **Stewardship** standing reflects how effectively they cultivate
  those they sponsor.

## Why this matters for autonomous agents

Because standing cannot be held by an agent, an agent can never bootstrap
governance authority through its own activity. All value an agent produces flows
to a responsible human sponsor, preserving the chain of legitimacy.
