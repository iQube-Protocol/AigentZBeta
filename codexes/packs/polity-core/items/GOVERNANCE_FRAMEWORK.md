# Governance Framework

**Version 1.0.0 · Ratified 2026-06-17 · Binds Constitution 1.0.0**

Governance authority belongs to **sovereign human citizens**. Agents are
governed; they do not govern.

## Who may govern

- Voting, sponsorship of sovereign actors, amendment of governance structures,
  and the granting of constitutional rights are reserved to **human citizens**
  (and the Bureau acting under citizen authority).
- Autonomous agents may **not** vote, sponsor sovereign actors, hold
  constitutional rights, or amend governance structures.

## Amendment authority

Constitutional amendments are recorded in the Amendment Records. Each ratified
framework carries a version; the machine-readable bundle at
`GET /api/polity-core/constitution` is the authoritative current set.

## Constitutional mismatch

Any agent whose Agent Passport binds to a superseded Constitution, Agent
Charter, or Delegation Framework version is **automatically suspended** until
re-bound to the current approved versions.
