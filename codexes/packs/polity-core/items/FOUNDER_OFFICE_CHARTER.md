# The Founder Office Charter

**Version 1.0.0 · Ratified 2026-06-17 · Status: ratified · Polity Core constitutional document — sub-metaCommons artefact**

> The Founder Office Charter is a **sub-charter within the metaCommons**. It is a
> constitutional-grade document, but not a top-level peer of the Polity
> Constitution. It sits **under** the [metaCommons Charter](./METACOMMONS_CHARTER.md)
> and is calibrated by the [Standing Charter](./STANDING_CHARTER.md). Founder
> Office is downstream of the metaCommons: it consumes the Commons signals the
> metaCommons surfaces and turns them into actionable opportunities.

## Capability Discovery, Opportunity Intelligence, and Venture Formation

## Purpose

Founder Office exists to help founders discover, evaluate, and transform latent
capability within the Polity into products, services, ventures, institutions, and
outcomes.

Founder Office is not primarily a software suite.

Founder Office is a capability discovery and opportunity intelligence service
powered by the metaCommons.

## First Principle

Founders do not create value from information alone.

Founders create value by identifying capability latent within information and
transforming that capability into useful work.

The purpose of Founder Office is to reduce uncertainty, reduce time-to-value, and
reduce the cost of discovering meaningful opportunities.

## The Founder's Challenge

Traditional founders operate in environments where:

- demand is difficult to observe,
- capability is difficult to discover,
- trust is difficult to establish,
- opportunity is difficult to validate.

The Polity exists to improve each of these conditions.

## Founder Office and the metaCommons

Founder Office is the primary institutional interface between founders and the
metaCommons.

The metaCommons surfaces:

- Intent Signals
- Demand Signals
- Capability Signals
- Opportunity Signals
- Standing Signals
- Proof of Work Potential

Founder Office transforms those signals into actionable opportunities.

## Proof of Work Potential

The most valuable opportunities are often invisible before they are realized.

Proof of Work Potential allows founders to discover:

- unmet needs,
- emerging needs,
- latent capability,
- undeveloped markets,
- unrealized opportunities.

Founder Office enables founders to act on those opportunities before traditional
markets recognize them.

## Standing and Founder Discovery

Standing does not determine who receives opportunities.

Standing calibrates confidence in signals.

Founder Office uses Standing to reduce uncertainty when evaluating:

- needs,
- opportunities,
- capabilities,
- partnerships,
- ventures.

## Founder Office Responsibilities

Founder Office shall:

- Surface meaningful opportunities.
- Reduce discovery costs.
- Reduce coordination costs.
- Reduce time-to-value.
- Increase the probability of successful venture formation.
- Increase the probability of useful capability deployment.

## Constitutional Principle

Founder Office exists to help citizens transform truthful information into useful
capability and useful capability into meaningful outcomes.

Its purpose is not extraction.

Its purpose is venture formation, capability development, and value creation
within the Polity.
