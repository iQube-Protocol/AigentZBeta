## Classification

  registry SoT with `primitive_type='ClusterQube'`).
- **Canonical name:** VentureQube.
- **Schema version:** `venture-iqube/v1.0`.
- **Product tiers:** **VentureQube Lite** = the v0.1→v0.4 operator wrapper — the
  standard / free path integrated with the aigentMe experience-model onboarding
  (single-venture idea incubation; position derived in the Lite experience-guide

  and multiple metaMe venture views in the Studio. The two coexist; Pro never
  replaces Lite — a Lite venture graduates into a Pro VentureQube.

## First Principle

A VentureQube is not a business plan, a pitch deck, or a startup profile. It is a
**living venture specification** — a sovereign, portable, agent-readable and
human-readable container whose purpose is to transform ideas into executable
ventures.

## The 13 Layers

1. **Identity** — name, slug, description, stage, T2-safe founder/passport/
   standing public refs, associated iQube ids. Stages: concept → validation →
   formation → launch → growth → scale → institution.
2. **Venture Thesis** — mission, vision, problem statement, consequence thesis,
   value proposition, category, industry/market tags, geographic scope.
3. **Intent** — founder intents, venture intents, citizen intents, commons
   intents.
4. **Signal Evidence** — evidence items (signal type/source, confidence,
   standing score, PoWP, timestamp) and the roll-up confidences: signal,
   opportunity, demand, capability.
5. **Customer Archetype** — who benefits / participates / pays / fulfils
   (citizen, creator, founder operator, executive, investor, institution),
   pain points, desired outcomes, willingness to pay, priority.
6. **Revenue Architecture** — revenue engines (subscription, services,
   licensing, commerce, transaction fees, intelligence services, venture
   participation), pricing models, prioritization, estimated revenue.
7. **Commercial Operating Model** — passport/citizen/creator/founder/executive/
   investor targets, conversion + acquisition assumptions, revenue / MRR / ARR
   targets, growth assumptions.
8. **Capability** — required / available / gaps / priorities.
9. **Resource** — required people, agents, tools, iQubes, capital.
10. **Execution** — phases (90/180/365-day), objectives, deliverables,
    dependencies, success metrics.
11. **Delegation (Agent Handoff)** — assignments to aigentMe, DevOn, Marketa,
    Venture Lab, Investor Office, with responsibilities, deliverables, success
    metrics.
12. **Outcome** — outcomes, Proof of Time Saved, standing changes, lessons
    learned (the venture learning loop).
13. **Governance & Institutional** — risk / sensitivity / accuracy /
    verifiability / standing confidence / PoWP / venture confidence; venture-lab
    status, investment status, commons visibility, institutional readiness +
    classification.

## Relationships

- **Standing** contributes to formation through capability + intent + veracity
  signals. Standing **does not determine venture success** — it **calibrates
  confidence in venture inputs**.
- **metaCommons** continuously evaluates a VentureQube against demand,
  capability, opportunity, and Proof of Work Potential — the intelligence layer
  for VentureQubes.
- **Proof of Work Potential** provides opportunity / capability / venture
  confidence estimates the VentureQube captures and operationalizes.
- **Proof of Time Saved** validates venture / capability / opportunity
  assumptions — the learning loop for both the venture and the Polity.

## Lifecycle

Intent → Opportunity → Validation → VentureQube creation → Execution → Venture →
Institution.

## Constitutional Principle (proposed — pending canonization)

The VentureQube exists to transform truthful information, demonstrated
capability, and collective intelligence into executable ventures capable of
creating meaningful outcomes within the Polity. It is the canonical artifact that
moves through the entire Founder Office ecosystem — and, eventually, the entire
Polity economy — bridging Founder Office, Venture Lab, aigentMe, DevOn, Marketa,
the metaCommons, and the future Investor Office.
