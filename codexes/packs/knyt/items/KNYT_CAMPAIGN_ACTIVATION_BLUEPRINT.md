# KNYT Campaign Activation Blueprint

## Purpose
This is the consolidated working blueprint for the metaKnyt Kickstarter activation campaign.

It serves two roles:
1. the live operating blueprint for the current campaign
2. a reusable activation model for Marketa and the Agentic Venture Studio in future launches

---

## Core framing
The metaKnyt Kickstarter campaign is not just a Kickstarter campaign. It is the first live proving ground for the broader AgentiQ / metaMe / KNYT / Venture Studio activation model.

### Canonical line
**The metaKnyt Kickstarter campaign should be treated as the first market-facing activation loop of the AgentiQ Venture Studio model: investor reactivation, community activation, partner activation, demand generation, monetization, progression, and signal capture — all feeding back into the studio and Runtime as proof of what the system can do.**
