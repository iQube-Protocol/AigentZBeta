## Core copy principles
### Always reinforce
- the campaign is live / ready now
- Kickstarter is the moment to act
- investor shelf perks are Kickstarter-exclusive
- only 21 of each investor tier are available across the full investor base
- the KNYT Codex materially expands what legacy investors may already own
- post-campaign investor benefits continue, but do not replace the value of backing now

### Never imply
- investors should wait until after Kickstarter
- the post-campaign 20% digital discount is a better move than backing now
- the KNYT Codex is just a duplicate of prior print and motioncomic ownership

- Scarcity line: Only 21 of each investor tier are available across the entire investor base.
- Ring-fence line: These shelf perks are Kickstarter-exclusive and do not carry forward into post-campaign retail.
- Codex line: The KNYT Codex materially expands what many legacy investors already own.
- Progression line: Complete shelves will position investors well for deeper Order and future PCS pathways.
- Anti-cannibalization line: Post-campaign investor benefits continue, but they do not replace the value of backing during the campaign itself.
