## Campaign mission

Reactivate the Metaiye Media investor base and convert that energy into visible early Kickstarter momentum for metaKnyt.

### Strategic mission
Use the campaign as the first live proving ground for:
- Marketa as an operator
- KNYT as a live activation world

**Reactivate investors first. Convert them through the Kickstarter now. Use the KNYT loop to deepen participation. Then widen into community, partners, and the broader Venture Studio narrative without diluting the campaign ask.**
