# KNYT Marketa Activation Plan

## Purpose
This document activates Marketa as the operating marketing lead for the metaKnyt Kickstarter campaign and its bridge into the broader AgentiQ / metaMe / Venture Studio launch.

## Two planning layers
### Layer A — immediate campaign layer
Focus on investor reactivation, CRM cleanup, campaign messaging, channel strategy, content schedule, pre-launch / launch / live campaign / close sequence, and conversions into Kickstarter support and Runtime participation.

### Layer B — macro studio layer
Use the campaign as proof that the Venture Studio model can activate investors, community, partners, and net-new audiences while generating monetization and signal that feed back into the ecosystem.

## What Marketa owns
- investor reactivation strategy
- campaign messaging hierarchy
- activation calendar and sequencing
- channel plan across email, SMS, Qriptopian, KNYT, Runtime, social, and Tasks & Rewards
- content brief pipeline
- partner activation logic
- signal review and campaign iteration

## Core audiences
1. legacy investors
2. existing KNYT community
3. strategic partners / aligned operators
4. net-new supporters
5. Venture Studio / ecosystem observers

## Immediate workstreams
1. CRM cleanup and segmentation
2. experience-matrix mapping
3. channel strategy by role
4. skill/tool ingestion requests
5. content architecture and schedule
6. KNYT / Runtime activation plan
7. partner extension once internal loop is working

## Core framing line
**Marketa’s immediate job is to reactivate the investor base and drive the metaKnyt Kickstarter campaign. Her strategic job is to do that in a way that proves the AgentiQ / metaMe / Venture Studio model in public, through real activation, real signal, and real monetization.**
