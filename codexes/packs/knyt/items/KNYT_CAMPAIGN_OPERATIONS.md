## 3. 30-day phase structure
### Days 1–2 — launch ignition
Wake up investors, clarify the tiers, create visible momentum.

### Days 3–7 — investor conversion and early momentum
Sharpen offer understanding, Codex value, Order/status framing, and advocacy prompts.

### Days 8–14 — community and social proof expansion
Widen beyond investors, deepen collector and shelf narratives, activate more KNYT participation.

### Days 15–21 — partner amplification and deeper narrative layer
Bring in partner amplification and the broader AgentiQ / Venture Studio frame without weakening the Kickstarter ask.

### Days 22–30 — urgency, retention, and bridge
Reinforce scarcity, close strong, then bridge supporters into Runtime / KNYT / post-campaign benefits.

## 5. Launch-day runbook
### Canonical launch-day line
**Today is about converting prepared belief into visible momentum.**

### Priorities in order
1. confirm the campaign page and links are correct
2. ensure investor-specific scarcity and ring-fence logic are reflected in all sends
3. send the launch wave cleanly across email, SMS, and social
4. ensure Qriptopian / KNYT / Runtime prompts are aligned
5. watch the first 2–4 hours of signal closely
6. convert early backers into visible social proof and advocacy

### Key checkpoints
#### T-minus 3 to 4 hours
- final link and page check
- confirm scarcity numbers and investor tier naming
- confirm launch email / SMS / social are queued
- confirm in-product prompts are visible

#### Launch moment
- send Launch Email
- send Launch SMS
- publish Launch Burst
- activate in-product launch prompts
- message highest-confidence champions if needed

#### T+1 hour
- assess opens
- assess clicks
- assess backing
- assess offer confusion or technical issues

#### T+2 to 4 hours
- post first momentum update if appropriate
- encourage early backers to share
- trigger share / recruit tasks where available
- route backers toward KNYT / Runtime continuation

## 6. Dashboard model
The dashboard must help answer:
1. Are we reactivating the right investors?
2. Are we converting attention into Kickstarter backing?
3. Are we turning backers into signal, advocacy, and deeper ecosystem participation?
4. Is this campaign proving the broader AgentiQ / metaMe / Venture Studio model?

### Daily dashboard questions
- Which cohort moved most today?
- Which cohort stalled today?
- Which channel produced the strongest action?
- Which message angle is converting best?
- Are Top KNYT Shelf or Zero KNYT moving faster?
- Are people only clicking, or actually backing?
- Are backers entering Runtime / KNYT follow-through?
- Which investors deserve faster follow-up now?
- Which partners deserve deeper follow-up now?
- Which contacts should be deprioritized for the moment?
- What should change tomorrow?

### Dashboard rule
The dashboard should not only report results. It should actively support dynamic sequencing decisions.
