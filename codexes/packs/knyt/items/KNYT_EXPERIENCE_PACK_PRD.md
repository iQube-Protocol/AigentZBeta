## 1. Purpose

The KNYT Cartridge is the first live world of the AgentiQ alpha ecosystem. It exists to do five things at once:

1. Ignite demand and attention
2. Convert awareness into patronage and belonging
3. Generate downstream participation and preference signals
4. Operationalize PCS through visible progression
5. Demonstrate a contained tokenized economy through $KNYT

KNYT is not just content. It is the first live cartridge world where users can move from outsider to patron, from patron to participant, and from participant to contributor or steward.

---

## 2. Strategic role in the alpha

| Role | What it does |
|------|-------------|
| Demand ignition | Gives users a compelling reason to enter the ecosystem |
| Signal layer | Captures affinity, preference, participation depth, remix intent, contribution readiness, loyalty |
| PCS proving ground | Makes visible the path from passive awareness to active contribution |
| Contained economy lab | Shows how a cartridge hosts its own local reward economy without forcing global tokenomics |

---

## 3. Core thesis

KNYT uses a **dual-axis experience system**:

- **Patronage Axis** — tracks movement through the Order
- **PCS Axis** — tracks movement through increasing agency, participation, and contribution

Together these axes guide users from outsider to patron, from patron to participant, and from participant to sovereign contributor.

---

## 4. Macro experience intent

Guide people from initial awareness of KNYT into meaningful patronage, participation, contribution, and ultimately stewardship or franchise-level alignment.

## 8. KNYT Experience Model

### Structural tier

**Products:** QriptoGraphic Novel, digital still editions, motion comics, KNYT cards, KNYT Codex access, KNYT Cartridge access, order collectibles, 21 Sats franchise assets

**Services:** onboarding, lore/world guidance, participation flows, voting, curation, remix prompts, correspondent/community contribution, creator guidance, order progression guidance

**Utilities:** wallet, persona, codex access, runtime cartridge access, participation tools, reward display/tracking, progression tracking, contributor pathway signals, messaging/community

**Channels:** metaMe Runtime, Qriptopian, email, social surfaces, community surfaces, future satellite channels

**Content:** lore, storyworld assets, KNYT media, Qriptopian articles, participation prompts, creator prompts, onboarding copy, progression/reward explanations

**Data:** patronage tier, participation history, reward state, PCS stage, signal history, preference indicators, next-best-step inputs, contributor readiness flags

### Emotional tier

| Layer | Elements |
|-------|----------|
| Personalization | Order-aware prompts, role-aware next-best-steps, creator/contributor-tailored invitations |
| Loyalty | Order progression, repeated participation loops, recognition, memory of involvement |
| Purpose | Belonging to the world, participating in living canon, progressing toward sovereignty |
| Trust levers | Provenance, visible world rules, transparent reward logic, clear progression paths, contained token economy boundaries |

### Transactional tier

**Commerce:** collectibles, access, upgrades, order progression purchases, premium paths, franchise opportunities

**Rewards:** $KNYT, unlocks, status markers, access expansion, contribution recognition, loyalty reinforcement

**Tokenomics:** $KNYT as KNYT-local reward and participation rail, contained cartridge treasury, participation and contribution incentives, experimental PCS economics within KNYT only

### Governance overlay

- Role-based access by patronage stage and PCS stage
- Contribution eligibility rules
- Reward eligibility rules
- Remix permissions
- Correspondent/worldbuilder permissions
- Stewardship thresholds
- Franchise-level access boundaries
- Trust and readiness requirements

## 12. Reward logic

**Principle:** $KNYT remains fully contained within the KNYT cartridge.

Rewardable action classes:
- First meaningful interaction
- Repeat participation streaks
- Curation events
- Remix actions
- Contributor submissions
- Correspondent actions
- Loyalty behaviors
- Patronage progression behaviors
- World-supportive stewardship behaviors

**Critical distinction:**
- QriptoCent (Q¢) = base system and operational transaction rail
- $KNYT = KNYT-local participation and contribution reward rail

This must remain explicit in all UX, docs, and launch language.

## 16. Alpha success criteria

KNYT alpha succeeds if:
- Users can move from outsider or Acolyte into visible participation
- Signal capture is meaningful and legible
- Patronage and PCS progression feel connected
- $KNYT reward logic is visible and motivating
- KNYT feels like a coherent world, not just a media page
- The cartridge demonstrates how a contained local economy sits on top of the wider stack
- The handoff from KNYT participation into broader sovereignty/contribution pathways is clear

---

## 17. Risks

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| KNYT becomes a content container instead of a live world | Prioritize participation mechanics, signals, and progression visibility |
| Patronage progression overwhelms PCS progression | Keep both axes visible and distinct |
| $KNYT gets confused with Q¢ | Always render base rail and local economy separately |
| Apex states feel purely paid rather than earned | Keep stewardship dependent on behavior, contribution, and trust — not just patronage |
