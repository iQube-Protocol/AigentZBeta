# KNYT Launch-Day Runbook + Partner Activation Brief

## Purpose
Provide the final two execution layers for the campaign:
1. launch-day checklist / run-of-show
2. partner activation brief and outreach structure

---

## Launch-day objective
Make sure the campaign goes live with:
- investor activation
- clear urgency
- coherent channel sequencing
- visible early momentum
- clean handoff into KNYT / Runtime / Tasks & Rewards

### Canonical launch-day line
**Today is about converting prepared belief into visible momentum.**

---

## Launch-day priorities in order
1. confirm the campaign page and links are correct
2. ensure investor-specific scarcity and ring-fence logic are reflected in all sends
3. send the launch wave cleanly across email, SMS, and social
4. ensure Qriptopian / KNYT / Runtime prompts are aligned
5. watch the first 2–4 hours of signal closely
6. convert early backers into visible social proof and advocacy

---

## Pre-launch-day readiness check
### Campaign infrastructure
- Kickstarter URL verified
- campaign page verified live and accurate
- investor-only tiers verified
- scarcity counts verified for Top KNYT Shelf and Zero KNYT

### Messaging and assets
- Email 1 sent
- Email 2 sent
- Social Blast 1 posted
- Social Blast 2 posted
- Launch Email approved
- Launch SMS approved
- Launch social burst approved

### In-product surfaces
- Qriptopian prompts live
- KNYT prompts live
- Runtime next-best-action prompts live
- Tasks & Rewards prompts ready

### CRM / tracking
- investor cohort tags active
- high-confidence outreach list ready
- priority SMS list ready
- dashboard or minimum scorecard live
- partner outreach list ready

---

## Launch-day run-of-show
### T-minus 3 to 4 hours
- final link and page check
- confirm scarcity numbers and investor tier naming
- confirm launch email and SMS are queued
- confirm social launch burst is queued
- confirm Qriptopian / KNYT / Runtime prompts are visible
- confirm dashboard / tracking is open and watched

### T-minus 2 hours
- finalize list of high-confidence investors for immediate attention
- prepare direct outreach notes for top champions if needed
- confirm partner outreach timing and priority

### T-minus 1 hour
- final proof on launch email, SMS, and social burst
- confirm all CTAs point to Kickstarter first
- confirm no copy implies waiting until after campaign

### Launch moment
- send Launch Email
- send Launch SMS
- publish Launch Burst social
- activate in-product launch prompts
- send direct notes to highest-confidence champions if needed

### T+1 hour
- assess opens, clicks, backing, offer confusion, and technical issues

### T+2 to 4 hours
- post first momentum update if appropriate
- encourage early backers to share
- trigger share / recruit task logic
- route backed users toward KNYT / Runtime continuation

### T+4 to 8 hours
- review performance by cohort, channel, and offer
- decide whether next emphasis should be scarcity, Codex value, Zero status, or community proof

---

## Launch-day decision rules
### If opens are high but clicks are weak
Sharpen CTA and investor-tier framing.

### If clicks are high but backing is weak
Clarify tier differences, reinforce campaign-only scarcity, simplify the decision.

### If Top KNYT Shelf is moving but Zero is weak
Reinforce Zero KNYT recognition and Order positioning.

### If Zero is moving but Top KNYT is weak
Emphasize foundational collector value and Codex uplift.

### If both are weak
Simplify message to: campaign is live, act now, choose your investor tier.

---

## Partner activation objective
Partners are not the first ignition source. Investors are.

Partners are the second-wave amplifier once the internal KNYT loop is moving.

### Canonical rule
**Prove the internal loop first, then extend it through partners.**

---

## Partner tiers
### Tier 1 — signal-boost partners
Repost on social, include short mention in email or newsletter, link to Kickstarter.

### Tier 2 — editorial / content partners
Co-publish articles or features, run spotlight pieces, help explain why the campaign matters beyond Kickstarter.

### Tier 3 — community / distribution partners
Email their list, share into communities, encourage participation and discovery.

### Tier 4 — strategic ecosystem partners
Amplify the campaign and help validate the broader Venture Studio model.

## Partner launch-week sequence
### Day 1–2
No broad partner push before the campaign has internal investor motion unless the partner is extremely high value and ready immediately.

### Day 3–7
Activate first outreach in the supplied relationship order.

### Day 8–14
Expand into broader community / distribution partners and deeper content asks.

### Day 15+
Use stronger macro / Venture Studio framing with strategic partners.

---

## Canonical shorthand
**Launch day is about investor ignition and clean momentum. Partner activation is about extending a loop that is already working, not compensating for one that is not.**
