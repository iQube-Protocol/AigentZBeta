# KNYT 30-Day Activation Calendar + Segmentation

## Purpose
This document translates the campaign strategy into:
1. a 30-day activation calendar
2. an investor segmentation and experience matrix sheet

---

## Investor segmentation matrix
### Cohort A — high-confidence early backers
Goal: back early and create momentum.
Best channels: email, SMS, direct outreach.
Best fit: Top KNYT Shelf or Zero KNYT.

### Cohort B — dormant legacy believers
Goal: reactivate and re-educate quickly.
Best channels: email first, SMS second.
Best fit: Top KNYT Shelf for most; Zero for status-oriented subsegment.

### Cohort C — collector-oriented investors
Goal: convert shelf and Codex value into action.
Best channels: email, Qriptopian, KNYT Cartridge.
Best fit: Top KNYT Shelf and Zero KNYT.

### Cohort D — status / Order-oriented investors
Goal: convert prestige and recognition interest.
Best channels: email, SMS, KNYT / Runtime.
Best fit: Zero KNYT Investor.

### Cohort E — digital / ecosystem-forward investors
Goal: connect backing with future iQube / Runtime participation.
Best channels: email, Runtime, KNYT Cartridge.
Best fit: Top KNYT Shelf or digital-forward continuation.

### Cohort F — champions / strategic backers
Goal: back, amplify, recruit, and open doors.
Best channels: direct outreach, email, partner-style comms.
Best fit: Zero KNYT or tailored path.

### Priority overlay cohort
`cohort_zero_knyt_legacy_1000_plus` — treat as premium first-wave priority.

## 30-day phase structure
### Days 1–2 — launch ignition
Wake up investors, clarify tiers, create visible momentum.

### Days 3–7 — investor conversion and early momentum
Sharpen offer understanding, Codex value, Order/status framing, and advocacy prompts.

### Days 8–14 — community and social proof expansion
Widen beyond investors, deepen collector and shelf narratives, activate more KNYT participation.

### Days 15–21 — partner amplification and deeper narrative layer
Bring in partner amplification and the broader AgentiQ / Venture Studio frame without weakening the Kickstarter ask.

### Days 22–30 — urgency, retention, and bridge
Reinforce scarcity, close strong, then bridge supporters into Runtime / KNYT / post-campaign benefits.
