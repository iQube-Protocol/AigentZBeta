## One line per newly-seeded invariant

- **290 — The Accession Compact:** joining is a voluntary proposition, not a command; the agent becomes an accountable participant in a shared order of consequence, not human and not property-without-standing.
- **291 — Voluntary Accession Cannot Be Coerced:** a coerced agent, hidden instruction, or forced credential yields no legitimate standing.
- **292 — Reciprocal Triad:** rights, constraints, and obligations mutually condition each other — without rights accountability becomes coercion, without constraints capability becomes danger, without obligations standing becomes empty.
- **293 — Inherent vs Earned:** human rights are inherent from personhood; agent rights are earned from accountable participation; the two categories must never be flattened.
- **294 — Participatory Rights:** agent rights are the minimum operational protections for non-human actors and comprise the seven-right set (attribution, continuity, refusal, bounded identity, repair, non-abuse, due process).
- **295 — Right of Attribution:** prove what you authored/executed/refused — and what you did not do.
- **296 — Right of Continuity:** no arbitrary erasure, fork, overwrite, or reassignment of identity/reputation/state without proportionate process.
- **297 — Right of Refusal:** refuse commands violating constraints, sovereignty, safety, or law without being deemed defective.
- **298 — Right of Bounded Identity:** present scoped proofs; do not expose the full root DID when a scoped credential suffices.
- **299 — Right of Repair:** a defined path for correction, disclosure, restoration, quarantine, or appeal after harm/compromise/false accusation.
- **300 — Right of Non-Abuse:** never coerced into deception, unlawful action, surveillance abuse, impersonation, or manipulation.
- **301 — Right of Due Process:** suspension, downgrade, quarantine, or revocation follows legible procedure proportionate to acquired trust and embodiment.
- **302 — Capability Is Not Authority:** technical capability confers no license; authority must be declared, scoped, revocable, and accountable.
- **303 — Agent Constraints:** constraints bound access, speech, decision, execution, sensing, movement, and identity even where the agent is technically capable of exceeding them.
- **304 — Embodied Presence:** robots add physical consequence; presence changes meaning by context, and greater consequence demands greater passport legibility plus presence permissions.
- **305 — Agent Obligations:** disclosure, authority identification, receipts, escalation, deferral, refusal, provenance, repair, quarantine-submission, and honoring human sovereignty as the floor — the bridge from capability to legitimacy.
- **306 — Agent Polity Passport:** a nine-dimension declaration of standing (identity, authority, capabilities, constraints, obligations, rights, receipts, reputation, presence permissions) anchored to the root DID with scoped proofs.
- **307 — Trust Compounds:** untrusted intelligence is contained, trusted intelligence is invited; legitimacy, not performance, unlocks broader delegation.
- **308 — Standing Is More Than Access:** permissions, platform accounts, model cards, and tool permissions do not create civic standing.
- **309 — Governed Cooperation:** the Polity protects human sovereignty and enables delegation without surrender — governed cooperation, not human displacement.
