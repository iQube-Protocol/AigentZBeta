Three follow-on increments landed after the N1+N2+N3 pilot skeleton.

## Increment 3b — commercial tier-gating on the three experiences

`FinancialServicesTab` now reads the existing plan (`GET /api/billing/plan`) and
gates by tier — **no parallel tiering**:

- **Constitutional Preview** (Run shadow) — open to all.
- **Founder Office** (form/accept/authorize an agreement + authoritative run) —
  gated on `ventureLabAccess`; an upgrade banner shows + the controls disable
  otherwise.
- **Advanced** — gated on `venture_tier` pro/elite (labelled).

Fails closed to preview-only if the plan can't be read; admins get full access
via the plan route's admin override. Each experience card shows an Active / tier
badge.

## Increment 2b — the pipeline is no longer a stub

- **Grounded executor.** `financialIntelligenceExecutor` is now
  invariant-grounded (async, an injected grounding fn wired to the engine's
  `groundReasoning`): real **sources** (invariant statements) + **evidence refs**
  (invariant ids), confidence calibrated to the evidence count. Un-grounded still
  honestly fails F-201/F-202 (never a fabricated pass). The grounding fn is
  injected so the executor stays node-testable.
- **Live-wired pipeline steps.** Step 4 reads real delegate standing
  (`readDelegateStanding`); step 5 checks the agreement's forbidden-action
  envelope (refuses at step 5 in authoritative mode); steps 11/12 cite the
  governing + evidence invariants for **real Reach accrual** (`citeInvariants`) —
  authoritative only. **Shadow observes, never mutates** (CFS-017): in shadow the
  accrual is recorded as "would accrue," not performed.

## P3 — money-moving Domains 1/2 gating (spend cap + settlement binding)

- `SettlementTerms` type + **`spendWithinCap`** (pure) on the agreement layer:
  money movement **requires** an enforced `valueCeiling` on the delegated
  authority; a settlement with no ceiling, or an amount over it, is **refused**.
- Pipeline **step 9 (Settlement)** enforces the cap when `settlementTerms` are
  present — refuses at step 9 in authoritative mode. Domain 3 (read-only, no
  terms) still skips settlement.
