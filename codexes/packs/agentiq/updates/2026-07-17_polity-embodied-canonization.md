## One line per new invariant

- **260** — The next AI-governance problem is unbounded assertion, not misinformation.
- **261** — The five tiers of operational truth: ungrounded → grounded → verified → auditable → canonical.
- **262** — A FACT is a time-bound operational truth object carrying its own provenance/verification/supersession metadata.
- **263** — iQubes are assertion-integrity containers that make a model's claims governable, not omniscient.
- **264** — Presence is information; a robot's body is a claim in space that changes system state.
- **265** — The body is part of the assertion: robots emit sensor, spatial, motor, role, authority, and symbolic assertions.
- **266** — The Canonical FACT Ledger (iQubes bind meaning→context, DVN context→receipt, Bitcoin receipt→time) is the governance spine — and the crystal's own provenance model.
- **267** — Risk scoring is the routing logic of the veracity stack; required trust scales with consequence.
- **268** — Embodied PoWP/PoTS must count time saved, displaced, externalized, and time-to-repair.
- **269** — Every consequential AI assertion must carry a legible state to prevent assertion collapse.
- **270** — Symbolic participation is not canonical status: canonizing a role never canonizes an inner realization (Gabi).
- **271** — Ungoverned embodied AI creates institutional hallucinations — apparent authority without possession.
- **272** — Governance turns on making intelligence accountable to human meaning, not on raw intelligence.
- **273** — Governed presence: legible, permissioned, bounded, risk-scored, receipted, sovereignty-accountable.
- **274** — Presence is a sovereignty event; embodied AI enters sovereignty, not merely space.
- **275** — Presence operates in four layers: objective, subjective, conscious, subconscious.
- **276** — The same body in a different space is a different assertion; context governs the meaning of presence.
- **277** — iQubes (PresenceQube / RobotQube) are presence-calibrating containers that prevent presence becoming future repair.
- **278** — Presence risk-scoring spans eight dimensions (sovereignty, vulnerability, authority, surveillance, emotional, physical, institutional, repair).
- **279** — Platform presence optimizes for capture (conduct), polity presence for agency.
- **280** — Citizenship requires legible presence; keep civic assistance distinct from civic enclosure.
- **281** — Child safety requires bounded delegation: a role in the child's environment, never sovereignty over the child.
- **282** — Consequential presence must be receipted; the presence receipt joins the operational fact base.
- **283** — Accountable action begins with governed presence — the first act of embodied AI is appearing.
