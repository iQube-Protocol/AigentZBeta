## Executive summary

Operation Chrysalis 2.0 transforms the Human Agency System from an AI-enabled platform into the world's first **constitutional computing** and **constitutionally sovereign** computing environment, by:

- operationalizing Invariant Intelligence across every reasoning surface and every rendering surface;
- instituting normative constitutional order across the complete operating model;
- internalizing the complete innovation lifecycle within Aigent Z and the AgentiQ harness;
- transforming external AI models into interchangeable constitutional inference providers;
- establishing provider sovereignty and sovereign survivability.

The result is a constitutionally complete Human Agency System capable of reasoning, developing, deploying, operating and continuously improving within its own constitutional framework.

## Mission

Create the first Human Agency System capable of **constitutional reasoning, constitutional computing, constitutional development, constitutional operations, constitutional learning, and constitutional sovereignty** — without requiring external AI operating environments.

## Constitutional Principles

1. **Invariant Intelligence** — every reasoning surface and every rendering surface SHALL be governed by Invariant Intelligence.
2. **Constitutional Development** — all capability development SHALL occur through Aigent Z.
3. **Provider Sovereignty** — no external AI provider SHALL become constitutionally indispensable.
4. **Sovereign Survivability** — the Human Agency System SHALL remain operational in the absence of any frontier AI provider. A sovereign fallback capability SHALL exist using open-weight models. Operational quality may degrade; constitutional operation shall not.
5. **Canonical Ontology** — canonical ontology SHALL take precedence over conversational inference. Invariant Intelligence SHALL resolve names, entities, primitives, protocols, and relationships before reasoning.
6. **Constitutional Learning** — every interaction SHALL improve the constitutional capability of the Human Agency System.

## Third Constitutional Agent — Constitutional Integration

**Mission:** constitutional integration. **Responsibilities:** compare outputs, validate invariants, detect architectural drift, maintain constitutional consistency, consolidate implementation, prepare integration PRs, benchmark capability improvement. *(Implementation note: a VERIFICATION role — canaries, drift benchmarks, continuity tests, integration review — not a third development stream.)*

## Success criteria

**Continuity:** constitutional reasoning, historical architectural understanding, VentureQube/PortfolioQube/Experience Guide consistency, Alethean behavioural continuity, and strategic conversations all continue without reconstruction.
**Improvement (measured):** reasoning quality, implementation quality, development velocity, Time-to-Value, reduction in context drift — from repository/runtime/registry/deployment visibility, Studio skill invocation, direct code visibility, Invariant Intelligence, and Consequence Engineering.
**Sovereignty:** the Human Agency System can reason, plan, develop, deploy, operate, and learn using only Aigent Z, AgentiQ, and sovereign inference providers.

## Final acceptance test — the Chrysalis Test

The Human Agency System successfully develops strategy, performs constitutional reasoning, engineers consequences, generates Implementation Packs, develops capabilities, deploys capabilities, validates outcomes, generates receipts, learns operationally, and improves future constitutional reasoning — **without leaving the Human Agency System**. External AI models function solely as interchangeable constitutional inference providers.

## The Chrysalis Contract

Every enhancement to the Human Agency System must improve at least one constitutional capability: Constitutional Computing, Constitutional Development, Constitutional Operations, or Constitutional Learning. If it improves none of these, it does not belong in Operation Chrysalis. *(This is Law X — every evolution must strengthen the invariant ontology — restated at program level; recorded as equivalent in CFS-009.)*

## Constitutional North Star

The Human Agency System SHALL become the primary environment in which its own future evolution is conceived, reasoned about, consequence-engineered, designed, implemented, validated, deployed, operated and continuously improved.

## Appendix B — Sequencing (constitutional)

The three strands share a dependency spine: Strand 2 consumes Strand 1's reasoning services; Strand 3 consumes both. Per the sequencing corollary of Law XV (`inv.constitutional.078`), build order is constitutional data: **contracts single-authored first (one agent), adoption parallelized second (two agents against frozen contracts), verification continuous (the Integration Agent)**. Deployment-ownership transfer is operator-approval-gated per Law XI in every phase of this PRD — deploys are consequence-bearing acts; autonomy is ratified explicitly or not at all.
