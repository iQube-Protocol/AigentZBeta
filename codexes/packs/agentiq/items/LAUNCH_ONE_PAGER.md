# AgentiQ Alpha — One-Pager

## What AgentiQ is

AgentiQ is a sovereign AI ecosystem, not just another AI platform. It combines an upstream build layer for contributors, a governed platform layer for intake and registry operations, a personal sovereignty layer for user goals and experience progression, and a first live world where participation produces real signal and contained economic reward. The alpha is designed to prove that these layers can work together as one governed loop.

---

## The closed loop in plain language

AgentiQ Alpha turns contribution into experience and experience back into better contribution. Builders package supply in AgentiQ OS, the platform ingests and validates it, Studio composes it into usable experiences, metaMe delivers those experiences to people, KNYT captures participation and reward, users progress through PCS, and the resulting signal feeds future supply decisions.

```text
build → ingest → compose → experience → earn → progress → feed back
```

---

## The four cartridges

### AgentiQ OS
AgentiQ OS is the public upstream build layer. It is where contributors package ToolQubes, SkillQubes, WorkflowQubes, and ConnectorQubes and submit them into the Registry Ingestion Factory.

### AgentiQ
AgentiQ is the governed platform layer. It runs the Registry Ingestion Factory, Registry operations, orchestration logic, and the private workflows that turn incoming supply into trusted, composable platform assets.

### metaMe
metaMe is the personal sovereignty and experience layer. It governs the user's goals, ladder, matrix, next-best-pathway logic, and the Runtime and Studio surfaces through which experiences are delivered and progressed.

### KNYT
KNYT is the first live world in the alpha. It is the contained proving ground for participation, PCS progression, remix/contribution cues, and the first local reward economy built on $KNYT.

---

## The two economies

### Q¢ — base operational rail
Q¢ (QriptoCent) is the base transaction and settlement rail for the platform. It underpins pricing, metering, and operational flows across AgentiQ OS and the platform layer.

### $KNYT — contained cartridge economy
$KNYT is the local economy inside the KNYT cartridge. It is used for participation and contribution rewards in the alpha and is intentionally contained so PCS economics can be tested without locking the whole ecosystem into one global token model.

---

## What alpha means

This alpha is a closed-loop proof, not a full public launch. The goal is to show that upstream contribution, governed intake, Studio composition, Runtime delivery, participation signal, PCS progression, and contained reward can operate as one coherent system.

What is not in scope for alpha: full public marketplace maturity, finalized ecosystem-wide tokenomics, or the full trust-scoring and risk/value research stack.
