The seed crystal for the Invariant Ontology. Where philosophy becomes engineering, where research becomes implementation, and where every future capability ultimately traces its lineage.

Each invariant is expressed in its simplest canonical form. The machine-readable twin of this document is `canonical-invariants.seed.json` (same directory) — the file the Phase 1 ontology ingestion loads. **The two must be kept in lockstep**; the JSON is the ingestion source of truth, this document is the human canon.

147. *(reasoning · law)* **The three strata** — Invariant Intelligence has three strata by role: **Structural** (ontology — what is fundamentally true; properties of reality, independent of observer/computation/representation) · **Invariant Representations** (interface — how a structural invariant becomes computationally accessible: language, mathematics, code, graphs, images, DNA, world models, forms not yet invented) · **Constitutional** (governance — how a system justifies, trusts, governs, operationalizes that truth). Representation is **not a type of invariant** but the interface; a structural invariant becomes usable only through a representation, and the representation is never the invariant. *(Corrects the co-equal-"type" grouping in 126; the `inv.representation.*` family governs the interface, it is not a type of structural truth. ROLE decomposition — not to be equated with the CFS-019 Layer axis, the function axis, or the origin axis.)*
148. *(epistemology · law)* **The architecture-limitation principle** — the inability of a particular computational architecture to faithfully represent or reason over an invariant does not diminish the invariant's existence or validity; it reveals a limitation of the architecture, not of the invariant. Alphanumeric text is today's dominant representation, not the substrate. *(Explains LLM spatial-reasoning gaps, human high-dimensional-math limits, the emergence of world models. "Everything reaches the LLM as tokens" is true of the implementation, not the theory.)*
149. *(epistemology · principle)* **Operational truth vs absolute truth** — the three strata are themselves invariant fields that, in an absolute sense, overlap at their boundaries; where one ends and another begins is a matter of magnification and perspective. The Institute's separations are OPERATIONAL truths — sufficient and sharp at the working magnification — not claims of absolute non-overlapping boundaries. The definitions hold operationally, which is what the discipline requires. *(The register standing also operates in, 145. Contextualizes 082's field-independence as an operational abstraction.
