## Purpose
*(Reframed 2026-07-06: the CRP's real purpose has been discovered — it is no longer simply research.)*

The Constitutional Research Program (CRP) is **where the platform explores constitutional questions that are not yet implementation-ready** — a genuine research institute, the permanent laboratory that continuously generates the theoretical and experimental advances for Chrysalis 3.0 and beyond. It **deliberately feeds Chrysalis rather than becoming part of Chrysalis**: one stream builds the constitutional platform; the other expands the science behind it. Nothing in this charter blocks, gates, or sequences any Chrysalis 2.0 deliverable.

## Founding assets (transferred at charter)

The CRP does not start empty. Its founding assets are the platform's existing research instruments and results:

- **The Foundational Validation Series** (EXP-001 semantic fidelity, EXP-002 temporal fidelity, EXP-003 rediscovery savings) — protocols, artifacts, published canonical results, and the CFS-008/008a research record.
- **The Experiment Lab** (front-end runners, canonical hash-committed publication, DVN anchoring, the consolidated Report tab).
- **Designed-and-open experiments**: EXP-002b (perturbation mapping of the temporal coherence field) and the Chrysalis 2.0 sovereignty drill (EXP-004 territory — Chrysalis executes it as an acceptance test; the CRP owns its research interpretation).
- **The empirical-refinement discipline**: ratified constitutional content amended by experiment (CFS-012 §4, CFS-013 §7 precedents) — the CRP's method, already proven.

## Interface with Chrysalis

- **CRP → Chrysalis**: findings hand back as ratification proposals (the constitutional-evolution loop: the system reveals, humans ratify) and as designed experiments Chrysalis may execute as acceptance tests.
- **Chrysalis → CRP**: production telemetry, receipts, and validation events become research data (the flywheel is shared infrastructure).
- **Never**: a CRP question blocking a Chrysalis deliverable, or a Chrysalis deadline truncating a CRP investigation.

## Governance

Law XI applies in full: the CRP proposes; human constitutional authority ratifies. Research results publish through the canonical results pipeline (exact-text storage, sha256 commitment, DVN-anchorable receipts) — the trust model is identical to production because the flywheel is shared.
