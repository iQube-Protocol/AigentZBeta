# IRL-015 — Partner Cover Letter

**Accompanying the Current Research Package**
**Status:** Draft — operator's own voice; review and personalise before sending.

Dear Austin,

Thank you again for taking the time to review the early work from the Invariant
Research Lab. Your feedback has had a meaningful impact on the programme.

Rather than treating review as a validation step at the end of the process, we
have incorporated your observations directly into the evolution of the research
itself. Several important developments emerged from those discussions,
including:

- a clearer separation between engineering validation and scientific validation;
- the introduction of dedicated instrument validation experiments (IRV and IPV);
- a transition from lexical scoring toward abstraction-aware projection fidelity;
- stronger discipline around bounded claims and explicit limitations; and
- the expansion of the Discovery Engine into a recursive programme for
  discovering, comparing and validating domain-specific invariants.

Enclosed are the latest versions of the current research materials. These
represent the present state of the programme rather than a finished conclusion.
The overall direction remains encouraging, but we continue to treat every
finding as provisional until independently replicated.

As always, further critique is welcomed. One of the principles we are trying to
embody within the laboratory is that review should improve the science rather
than simply evaluate it.

Thank you again for helping us move the programme in that direction.

Kind regards,

**Dele Atanda**
Founder, metaMe Invariant Research Lab (IRL)
