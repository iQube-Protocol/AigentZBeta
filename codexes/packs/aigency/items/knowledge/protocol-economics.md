## 3. QriptoCENT (Qc / Q¢)

### What it is
QriptoCENT is the ecosystem's Bitcoin-anchored micro-stablecoin. It is the base operating and pricing currency of the broader ecosystem — designed for deterministic pricing and settlement of very small units of value.

### What it's for
- Price tiny units of work (curation, inference, routing, contribution)
- Settle tiny units of value
- Reward small actions
- Account for knowledge-work events
- Support machine-to-machine and human-to-machine transactions at sub-dollar and sub-cent precision

### The canonical mental model
Think of Qc as:
- the pricing rail
- the settlement rail
- the unit of economic precision
- the stable baseline currency for the ecosystem
- the economic grammar of the system

### Why it exists
Traditional payment rails are clumsy for tiny rewards, micro-contributions, agentic actions, and high-frequency machine payments. Qc solves that by functioning as the stable unit of account and exchange rail.

### Qc and knowledge-work
Qc is the core pricing and settlement rail for micro knowledge-work — curation, summarization, ranking, evaluation, contribution, participation in a campaign loop, routing, small creative and social actions that are economically meaningful.

### Qc and machine payments
Qc supports a world where agents can price services in tiny increments, machines can settle with other machines, and ecosystems can account for high-frequency, low-value interactions.

### What Qc is NOT
- Not a meme coin
- Not a speculative franchise token
- Not a fandom badge
- Not a generic loyalty point
- Not "quality cent"
