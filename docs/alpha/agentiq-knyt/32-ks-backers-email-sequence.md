## Segmentation Rules (CRM / AVL)

**Segment A — unopened after Email 1**
Resend Email 1 with a different subject line.

**Segment B — opened but not clicked**
Send Emails 2 and 3 as normal, with slightly stronger CTA language.

**Segment C — clicked but not converted**
Move into Emails 4 and 5 with direct ask and clearer urgency.

**Segment D — converted / backed**
Suppress from hard conversion pushes. Move into:
- gratitude
- share ask
- continuity path
- ladder / matrix routing

**Segment E — bounced / unsubscribed / invalid**
Suppress, groom, and hold for canonization review before Stage 2. Aligns with hygiene gate in doc 31.
