# Human–Agent Adoption Engine Thesis

## Thesis
Humans are acquired through meaning, identity, emotion, and guided experience.
Agents are acquired through discoverability, trust, authority, pricing, and machine-legible utility.
Organizations adopt only when there is policy, accountability, and budget control.

The system must serve all three at once.

## Core architecture
- metaMe protects and authorizes the individual
- AgentiQ OS attracts and equips agents
- AgentiQ shapes deployable cartridges for organizations and industries
- Qc and iQubes accelerate and coordinate the system
- DVN and minting prove and finalize value

## Product implication
The engine should be expressed through living cartridges, not abstract platform claims.
