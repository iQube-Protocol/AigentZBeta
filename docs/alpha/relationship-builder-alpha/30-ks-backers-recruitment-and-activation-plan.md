# KS Backers Recruitment and Activation Plan

## Status
Immediate execution plan

## Purpose

This plan defines **KS Backers** as a seeded customer cohort inside the broader **KNYT KS campaign**, executed through **AVL Relationship Builder**.

It should not be run as a separate parallel campaign.

Instead, it should be managed as a distinct cohort within the shared KNYT KS campaign framework so that:
- investor, prospect, and KS backer flows remain structurally unified
- cohort-specific messaging and assets can still be tailored precisely
- reporting remains coherent at both campaign and cohort level
- Marketa can orchestrate all launch-wave communications from one Relationship Builder surface
- captured backers and backer-prospects can be routed directly into the KNYT Matrix, ladder, and post-campaign continuity paths

## Immediate strategy update

Because we now have an imported list of approximately 2,000 KS backers, the first task is no longer backer discovery.

The first task is:
- import the seed list into CRM
- create the KS Backers cohort
- tag and segment the records
- launch an email-first activation funnel immediately
- score response and engagement
- route engaged users into Matrix / ladder / NBE logic
- expand and enrich the cohort later using the prior organic/swap plan

No ad spend should be incurred initially.

## Placement in the campaign model

- Parent campaign: **KNYT KS Campaign**
- Cohort: **KS Backers**
- Initial source: imported CRM seed list (~2,000 known KS backers)

## Stage 1 — Seed cohort activation

### Objective
Engage the known KS backers immediately through email and begin routing them into KNYT relationship state.

### Required first actions
1. import list into CRM
2. create or confirm cohort tag: `ks_backers`
3. create seed sub-tag: `ks_backers_seed`
4. attach campaign tag: `knyt_ks_campaign`
5. create basic segmentation fields
6. prepare first email funnel
7. begin response scoring and engagement tracking

### Stage 1 channel strategy
Primary channel: **Email**

Do not start paid social yet.

Paid social can be introduced later once:
- the CRM import is live
- the first funnel has run
- performance signals exist
- social and SMS capabilities are better integrated into the Relationship Builder skill stack

## Email funnel structure

### 1. Awareness phase
Introduce the project clearly and warm the audience without overselling.

### 2. Interest phase
Show why the project exists, what makes it distinct, and why it is worth paying attention to.

### 3. Conversion phase
Invite direct support with clear but respectful urgency.

### 4. Follow-up sequence
Run mid-campaign updates, late reminders, final calls, and then a post-campaign continuity handoff.

## Stage 2 — Cohort enrichment and expansion

After the seed cohort has been activated and measured, expand and enrich it using:
- organic social
- creator swaps
- newsletter swaps
- Kickstarter-native updates
- later retargeting and social ads
- later SMS if integrated

These are Stage 2 tactics, not the initial motion.

## Relationship Builder surfaces to use

### Customer surface
Primary home for the cohort.

### KNYT Relationship Composer
Used to create cohort-specific email variants, follow-up messages, campaign updates, and post-campaign continuity actions.

### Reports
Used to track sends, opens, clicks, conversion signals, progression, and cohort expansion.

### QubeTalk
Support, escalation, and crossover coordination later.

## Matrix and ladder integration

Every engaged KS Backer should be routed into:
- customer-side Experience Matrix
- ladder state
- NBE logic
- post-campaign continuity

## Success criteria

This plan is successful when:
- the 2,000-person backer list is imported and cohort-tagged
- the KS Backers cohort is visible in AVL customer surface
- the email-first funnel launches immediately
- engagement and conversion signals are measurable
- recipients are routed into Matrix / ladder / NBE states
- the cohort is ready to be expanded through Stage 2 tactics

## One-line definition

The KS Backers Recruitment and Activation Plan turns a seeded list of known KS backers into an actively managed customer cohort inside the broader KNYT KS campaign, using email-first activation now and cohort expansion/enrichment later through the Relationship Builder.
