# AVL Customer Surface Addendum

## Purpose

The internal AVL surface must support both:
- partner relationship management
- customer / end-user relationship management

This creates a single relationship orchestration surface from which Marketa and the platform can manage communications, campaigns, progression, and next-best-experiences across both sides of the KNYT launch system.

## Why this matters

The evolved AVL model needs one place to manage:
- partner-facing comms
- investor-facing comms
- prospect-facing comms
- KS Backers comms
- post-campaign continuity comms
- customer ascension and ladder progression
- future Venture Lab pipeline candidacy

## Customer surface responsibilities

The AVL customer surface should manage:
- cohort creation and segmentation
- outbound communications
- campaign-wave targeting
- seeded cohort activation
- lifecycle tracking
- ladder progression
- Experience Matrix routing
- NBE management
- reporting
- continuity beyond the current launch wave

## Core cohorts

At minimum, the customer surface should support:
- Investors
- Prospects
- KS Backers
- Warm Social Engagers
- Post-Pledge Continuity
- Ladder / Pipeline Candidates

## KS Backers requirement

For the current KNYT KS campaign, KS Backers begins as an imported seed cohort of known backers.

The customer surface must support:
- import
- tagging
- segmentation
- email-first execution
- engagement scoring
- Matrix routing
- ladder progression
- canonization
- Stage 2 expansion readiness

KS Backers should be treated as a distinct customer cohort within the broader KNYT KS campaign, not as a separate side campaign.

## Ladder ascension and Venture Lab pipeline

The customer surface must not only track campaign engagement.

It must also monitor:
- which users are becoming deeper collectors
- which users show curator or correspondent potential
- which users look franchise-adjacent
- which users should become future Venture Lab pipeline candidates

## Internal navigation implication

Internal AVL mode should explicitly include:
- Overview
- Programs
- Composer
- Customers
- Partners
- Packs
- Reports
- QubeTalk
- Settings

## One-line definition

The AVL customer surface is the internal operating layer for managing customer cohorts, communications, progression, and Venture Lab pipeline visibility across the KNYT Program.
