# AVL Relationship Builder — KNYT Relationship Composer PRD

## Executive summary

AVL Relationship Builder is the venture-studio relationship orchestration surface.

It replaces the old Marketa Cartridge in product framing while keeping **Marketa** as the lead relationship agent.

Inside AVL, **KNYT Relationship Composer** becomes the first live program workspace. It uses Studio, Experience Matrix, Registry, workflows, and execution rails to compose partner and customer actions aligned to cohort, ladder state, journey state, and next-best-experience.

## Core product decisions

- AVL Relationship Builder replaces the old user-facing Marketa Cartridge concept.
- Marketa remains the lead relationship agent inside AVL.
- KNYT Relationship Composer is the first live program workspace.
- KNYT is an ongoing program.
- The live Kickstarter is Campaign Wave 1 of that broader KNYT program.
- Internal AVL must support both partner and customer communications.
- Customer progression, ladder ascension, and Venture Lab pipeline visibility are part of the internal operating model.

## KNYT Relationship Composer

The Composer is the Studio-powered action builder for the KNYT program.

Inputs include:
- program
- campaign wave
- cohort / individual / partner class
- ladder state
- Experience Matrix state
- NBE
- channel mix
- asset family
- offer / incentive type
- execution rail
- schedule

Outputs include:
- owned pack
- partner pack
- loyalty action
- affiliate / referral action
- workflow payload
- reporting hooks

## Internal customer surface requirement

Internal AVL must manage end-user and customer communications alongside partner communications. This gives the venture studio one surface from which to manage:
- investor campaigns
- prospect campaigns
- KS Backers cohort activation
- customer retention and continuity
- ladder ascension
- Venture Lab pipeline candidacy

## KNYT Program framing

KNYT should be structured as an ongoing program whose first major activation wave is the Kickstarter campaign. The close of the Kickstarter marks the end of the opening launch phase, not the end of KNYT.
