# AVL Continuity Addendum

## Purpose

This addendum clarifies that KNYT should be implemented as an ongoing program, not a one-off campaign tied only to Kickstarter.

## Core statement

KNYT is an enduring venture-studio program.
The live Kickstarter is **Campaign Wave 1** of that broader KNYT program.
The close of the Kickstarter marks the end of the opening launch phase, not the end of KNYT.

## Structural model

Use:
- Program
- Campaign Wave
- Always-On Action

Not:
- Campaign
- Launch
- End

## KNYT lifecycle

### 1. Launch activation wave
The Kickstarter period.

### 2. Conversion and continuity wave
Begins during the final stretch of Kickstarter and continues after close.

### 3. Expansion waves
New activation arcs after launch.

### 4. Evergreen program layer
Always-on KNYT engagement between and beyond campaign waves.

## Composer implication

KNYT Relationship Composer must support:
- program composition
- campaign-wave composition
- always-on relationship actions

## Data implications

Implement continuity-aware support for:
- `program_id`
- `campaign_wave_id`
- `always_on_action_type`
- continuity state
- reward visibility continuity
- NBE continuity

## Quiet strategic principle

KNYT should be treated as a long-lived human engagement system whose launch waves create intensity, but whose deeper design goal is continuity of participation, identity, contribution, and relationship progression over time.
